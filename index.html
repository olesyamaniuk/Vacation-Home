About the Game

Relax, Merge, Decorate

Vacation Home – Merge & Design is a casual, cozy game where you restore and style dreamy seaside homes. With every merge, you uncover tools, furniture, and decor to bring your ideal retreat to life. Enjoy the perfect balance of relaxing gameplay and creative freedom as you turn dusty cottages into paradise hideaways.

Merge Magic

Match & Evolve Items

Drag, drop, and combine identical objects to unlock more useful and beautiful tools, furniture, and decorations. Whether it’s fixing a pool or revamping a kitchen, each merge brings new possibilities.

Room by Room

Customize Every Corner
From outdoor patios to sunny bedrooms, choose your style and decorations to make every room Instagram-worthy. The game gives you full control of how your vacation dream home should look.
